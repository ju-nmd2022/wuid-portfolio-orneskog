About me:

My name is Charlie Örneskog and I'm a first year New Media Design student at Jönköping University.
I'm 32 years old and I have worked in the hospitality business for 13 years so food and wine has become a big interest of mine.

I also studied 'Digital Design' while studying at the Gymnasium back in 2006-2009 and it's really interesting to see how far the industry has come since then.
